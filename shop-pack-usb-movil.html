CRUDOLOGÍA IN PHYSICAL FORMAT


As you all know, these are difficult times for physical records due to the lack of CD players and vinyl record players, among others.

As there are still people who love physical records, those of us that like the tangible more than the digital (as is my case, I am very tired of the virtual, by the way), I have also decided to launch the album in a very cool format. This format uses very attractive and modern packaging while simultaneously combining the needs of both preferences: DIGI-USB MUSIC format.
